Mental Health Education

Learn about mental health, common mental illnesses, and how to improve your mental well-being.

Anxiety is a feeling of unease, such as worry or fear, that can be mild or severe. Everyone experiences anxiety at some point in their life, but if it starts to interfere with your daily life, it may be a sign of an anxiety disorder. There are several types of anxiety disorders, including generalized anxiety disorder, panic disorder, and social anxiety disorder.
Depression is a mood disorder that causes persistent feelings of sadness, hopelessness, and loss of interest in activities. It can affect how you think, feel, and behave, and can interfere with your daily life. There are several types of depression, including major depressive disorder, persistent depressive disorder, and seasonal affective disorder.
There are several things you can do to improve your mental well-being, including:
  • Practice self-care, such as eating healthy, getting enough sleep, and exercising regularly.
  • Engage in activities that you enjoy and that give you a sense of purpose.
  • Connect with others and build strong relationships.
  • Seek professional help if you are experiencing mental health problems.

Mindfulness Exercises

Take a few minutes to practice these mindfulness exercises:

  • Breathing exercise

    Find a comfortable seated position, close your eyes and take a deep breath in through your nose, holding it for a few seconds before exhaling through your mouth. Repeat this for several minutes, focusing only on your breath.

  • Body scan meditation

    Lie down on your back and close your eyes. Slowly scan your body from your toes to your head, focusing on each body part and how it feels. Spend a few minutes on each body part before moving on to the next.

  • Gratitude meditation

    Sit in a comfortable position, close your eyes and think of three things that you are grateful for. Spend a few minutes on each one, focusing on why you are grateful and how it makes you feel.
